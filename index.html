About Sunita

Captain Sunita "Suni" Williams is an American astronaut and United States Navy officer of Indian-Slovenian descent. She has spent a total of 322 days in space over multiple missions, holds the record for total cumulative spacewalk time by a female astronaut, and has completed seven spacewalks.

Born in Euclid, Ohio, Williams graduated from the United States Naval Academy in 1987 and became a helicopter pilot, logging more than 3,000 flight hours in more than 30 different aircraft.

Selected as an astronaut by NASA in 1998, Williams has contributed immensely to humanity's understanding of living and working in space.
